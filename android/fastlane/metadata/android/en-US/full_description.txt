Protect your valuable data with the next generation of security. Liso is a crypto-first, Web3-powered data vault, built to leverage blockchain-grade security and decentralized technology. Safeguard passwords, notes, photos, videos, documents, and all sensitive information with advanced cryptography, decentralized protocols, and strong privacy standards. With Liso, your data stays yours—secure, private, and under your control, free from ads and tracking.

Liso uses modern cryptographic techniques and Web3 frameworks—including end-to-end encryption, zero-knowledge proofs, and secure wallet integrations—to protect your digital life. Store login credentials, passwords, crypto wallets, seed phrases, 2FA/MFA codes, secure notes, cards, bank accounts, identity documents, and more in a decentralized, impenetrable vault.

Liso is an all-in-one crypto password manager, cross-device vault, and next-gen authenticator, merging Web3 and classic security for unmatched protection.

Key Features:
◆ Next-Gen Crypto Security: Blockchain and Web3 technologies eliminate single points of failure and maximize data protection.
◆ Military-Grade AES-256 + End-to-End Encryption: Traditional and decentralized cryptography for unmatched security.
◆ Decentralized Identity: Authenticate with secure wallets or decentralized sign-in—no link to your real-world identity.
◆ Offline/Air-Gapped Mode: Access your encrypted vault without internet—your data never leaves your device unless you choose.
◆ Blockchain-Backed Sync: Sync data across devices with decentralized, encrypted cloud storage.
◆ Fingerprint/Face Biometrics: Multi-factor authentication with cryptographic signers.
◆ 2FA/MFA Authenticator: Generate and store two-factor codes in your encrypted vault.
◆ Weak Password Detection: Find and strengthen vulnerable passwords.
◆ Password & Item-Level Encryption: Extra encryption for your most sensitive items.
◆ Web3 File Encryption: Encrypt files with robust crypto methods.
◆ Powerful Password Generator: Ultra-strong, unique passwords with cryptographically secure randomness.
◆ Free Encrypted & Decentralized Cloud Storage: Only you hold the keys.
◆ Secure Photo and Video Vault: Lock down your digital life with decentralized encryption.
◆ Cross-Platform Access: Use Liso on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
◆ Easy Migration: Import from Bitwarden, LastPass, Chrome, and others—then encrypt with latest crypto standards.
◆ 100% Zero-Knowledge: Only you can access your data—no one else, not even Liso.
◆ Sign Up with Crypto Wallets: Start instantly with wallet authentication—no email or password needed.

Liso outperforms legacy password managers (1Password, LastPass, Bitwarden, Enpass, Keeper, Dashlane, Roboform, NordPass) with superior security, privacy, and digital freedom.
