Vikela idatha yakho ebalulekile ngokuvikeleka kwesizukulwane esilandelayo. I-Liso iyivault yedatha ekhokhelwa yi-Web3 eqala i-crypto, eyakhelwe ukusebenzisa ukuvikeleka begreyidi ye-blockchain netheknoloji engahlelekile. Vikela amaphasiwedi, amanothi, izithombe, amavidiyo, amadokhumenti nayo yonke imininingwane ezwakalayo nge-cryptography ethuthukile, amaprotocol angahlelekile namazinga aqinile okuzimela. Nge-Liso idatha yakho ihlala eyakho - evikelekile, eyimfihlo futhi ngaphansi kokulawula kwakho, ikhululekile kuzikhangiso nasekulandeleni.

I-Liso isebenzisa amasu e-cryptographic amanje nama-Web3 framework - kuhlanganise ukuvikelwa ukusukela ekugcineni kuya ekugcineni, ubufakazi be-zero-knowledge nokuhlanganiswa kwama-wallet avikelekile - ukuvikela impilo yakho yedijithali. Gcina amacredential okungena, amaphasiwedi, ama-wallet e-crypto, imisho ye-seed, amakhodi we-2FA/MFA, amanothi avikelekile, amakhadi, ama-akhawunti ebhange, amadokhumenti obunikazi nokunye ku-vault engahlelekile engangenwa.

I-Liso iyimanaja wamaphasiwedi e-crypto eyodwa-yonke, ivault yamadivayisi aphesheya nesiqinisekisi sesizukulwane esilandelayo, ehlanganisa i-Web3 nokuvikeleka kwesiko ukuze kuvikeleke okungalingani.

Izici eziyinhloko:
- Ukuvikeleka kwe-Crypto kwesizukulwane esilandelayo: Itheknoloji ze-blockchain ne-Web3 zisusa amaphoyinti okwehluleka ayodwa futhi zandisa ukuvikelelwa kwedatha.
- I-AES-256 yebreyidi yamasosha + ukuvikelwa ukusukela ekugcineni kuya ekugcineni: I-cryptography yendabuko nengahlelekile ukuze kuvikeleke okungalingani.
- Ubunikazi obungahlelekile: Ziqinisekise ngama-wallet avikelekile noma ukungena okungahlelekile - akukho linki nobunikazi bakho bangempela.
- Imodi ye-Offline/Air-Gapped: Thola ivault yakho ekhodiwe ngaphandle kwe-inthanethi - idatha yakho ayikaze ishiye idivayisi yakho ngaphandle kokuthi ukhethe.
- Ukuvumelanisa okusekelwe yi-Blockchain: Vumelanisa idatha phakathi kwamadivayisi ngokugcinwa kwamafu okungahlelekile okukhodiwe.
- I-Biometrics yomunwe/ubuso: Ukuqinisekiswa okunezici eziningi nabasayina be-cryptographic.
- Isiqinisekisi se-2FA/MFA: Khiqiza futhi ugcine amakhodi amabili ezici ku-vault yakho ekhodiwe.
- Ukutholwa kwephasiwedi elibuthakathaka: Thola futhi uqinise amaphasiwedi asengozini.
- Ukuvikelwa kwephasiwedi nesezinga se-item: Ukuvikelwa okungeziwe kuzinto zakho ezwakalayo kakhulu.
- Ukuvikelwa kwefayela le-Web3: Khoda amafayela ngendlela ye-crypto eqinile.
- I-generator yephasiwedi enamandla: Amaphasiwedi aqinile, ahlukile ngokungahlelekile okuvikelekile nge-cryptographic.
- Ukugcinwa kwamafu okhululekile okukhodiwe nokungahlelekile: Wena kuphela unamakhiye.
- Ivault yezithombe namavidiyo evikelekile: Vala impilo yakho yedijithali ngokuvikelwa okungahlelekile.
- Ukufinyelela kwe-Cross-Platform: Sebenzisa i-Liso ku-desktop, ithebhulethi neselefoni.
- Ukuthuthela okulula: Ngenisa kusukela ku-Bitwarden, LastPass, Chrome nakwezinye - bese ukhoda ngamazinga e-crypto amasha kakhulu.
- I-100% Zero-Knowledge: Wena kuphela ungangena kudatha yakho - akekho omunye, ngisho ne-Liso.
- Bhalisa ngama-Wallet e-Crypto: Qala ngokushesha ngokuqinisekiswa kwe-wallet - ayidingeki i-imeyili noma iphasiwedi.

I-Liso idlula abaphathi bamaphasiwedi bendabuko (1Password, LastPass, Bitwarden, Enpass, Keeper, Dashlane, Roboform, NordPass) ngokuvikeleka okuphezulu, ubumfihlo nenkululeko yedijithali.
